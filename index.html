About Us

At 1970, The Foundation of Interfood: exporting Condensed Milk and Calf Milk Replacers from Holland to Italy and Greece. At 2010s and later, We look back, celebrating our 45th anniversary. Our past values still remain and our high level of service that brought us where we are today has a proven record. Currently trading 900,000 MT of dairy products globally with the trust and appreciation from both our customers and suppliers, Interfood is now anxious to further develop and grow the global dairy market.
