Should I make a table reservation?

We strongly advise to book a table although it is not compulsory, especially on Week-ends which are more busy days. You can make the Reservation in our reservation page of our Website. Click here to make the reservation.

When is a good time to visit if I don't have a lot of time?

It will be good time arriving early or dining late! Mostly, our restaurants may be the busiest time from 12:00PM - 2:00PM and 6:00PM - 8:00PM on weekdays. Weekends may be busy all day.

Do you have takeout? Delivery?

Almost our entire menu is available for takeout. You may place your order in advance by calling the restaurant directly or by ordering at the Order page of our website. Of course, we offer the delivery to your place. Click here to make Order or Delivery.

Do you do anything special for guests celebrating a birthday?

For birthday guests, we serve the special promotion. You can learn about that promtion in the Special Event Promotion page of our Webstie. Click here to visit the page which is described some of the promotions.

Is it Okay to bring babies and children?

Absolutely! If you need a kids' menu, high chair or booster seat, please ask your host or server. We have changing tables for infants in both men's and women's restrooms (or in our family restrooms). We also offer Wikki Stix for children 3 years or older to enjoy.

Do you have Wi-Fi?

Of Course!! We offer the Wi-Fi in all our restaurants.

Do you have a private parking?

We don't have private parking, but you can park your car on the street next to our restaurants. You can be provided for your transfer or taxi which will get you directly in front of our restaurants.

Can I bring my pet in?

Sorry, the Pets are not allowed in our restaurants.

Do you offer groups hosting?

Sure!! You can book any of our Private room and host your group there!

How can I be certain my booking's been received?

We'll send an instant confirmation email to the address used in your booking. If you book more than a day ahead, we'll also send a reminder 24 hours before your booking is due. You can reconfirm your booking using a link in your reminder email.

What happens if I don't reconfirm my booking?

Don't worry, your restaurant will still hold your booking without a reconfirmation.

What happens if I'm running late?

You have to call the restaurant. Every restaurant is different, but our restaurants will hold a booking for at least twenty minutes before reassigning your table.

How do I cancel a booking?

To help our restaurants manage their sittings we ask our diners to give at least 24 hours' notice of any cancellations. You can do this by calling your restaurant direct. If you need to cancel less than 24 hours before your dinner date, you can use the cancellation link within your reminder email (sent 24 hours before your reservation time). We also encourage our diners to follow this up with a call direct to the restaurant.

How much do I have to pay for my booking?

Absolutely nothing! All bookings made through our Websites are free to the consumer.

Why do you need my email address?

All bookings are automatically confirmed via email. Without an address to send the confirmation to the booking, it can't be accepted. We'll also use your email address to send reminders for any bookings made more than 24 hours in advance of your dining date.
