About Our Company

We invite you to take a look at our rich history, coming from a small local family business having grown into the trusted leading global dairy company we are to today

At 1970, The Foundation of Interfood: exporting Condensed Milk and Calf Milk Replaces from Holland to Italy and Greece.

At 80s, The organisation grew to 16 employees by the end of the decade. Their focus was to provide our European customers with the products of Skimmed Milk Powder and Whey powder. At the end of the decade Interfood also began exporting to countries outside of Europe.

At 2010s and later, We look back, celebrating our 45th anniversary. Our past values still remain and our high level of service that brought us where we are today has a proven record. Currently trading 900,000 MT of dairy products globally with the trust and appreciation from both our customers and suppliers, Interfood is now anxious to further develop and grow the global dairy market.
